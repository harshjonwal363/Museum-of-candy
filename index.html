Featured Exhibits

Journey through different eras of candy making

Ancient Sweets

Explore how ancient civilizations created the first confections using honey, fruits, and nuts. Discover Egyptian honey cakes, Roman fruit preserves, and medieval sugar sculptures.

  • Egyptian honey cakes
  • Roman fruit preserves
  • Medieval sugar sculptures

Industrial Revolution

Discover how mass production changed the candy industry forever during the 19th century. See original steam-powered machinery and learn about the first chocolate bars.

  • Steam-powered machinery
  • First chocolate bars
  • Penny candy stores

Modern Confections

Experience the colorful world of contemporary candy making and molecular gastronomy. Try 3D printed chocolates and liquid nitrogen treats in our interactive lab.

  • 3D printed chocolates
  • Liquid nitrogen treats
  • Interactive candy lab

Sweet History Timeline

Key moments in candy evolution

2000 BC

Ancient Beginnings

Ancient Egyptians create first honey-based sweets, setting the foundation for all future confections.

1000 AD

Sugar Arrives

Sugar arrives in Europe through trade routes, revolutionizing the sweetness available to confectioners.

1847

Chocolate Bar Born

First solid chocolate bar invented by Fry's, changing how people consume chocolate forever.

1900

Mass Production

Mass production brings candy to the masses, making sweet treats affordable for everyone.

2020

Modern Innovation

Modern molecular gastronomy revolutionizes sweets with new textures, flavors, and experiences.
